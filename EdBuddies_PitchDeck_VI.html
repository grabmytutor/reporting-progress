Mission

EdBuddies.ai is a Singapore-based vertical SaaS and operational infrastructure company digitising the highly fragmented tuition and enrichment sector across Southeast Asia.

The company provides a unified operating platform powering: recurring billing, scheduling, attendance, parent communication, and administrative workflows for tuition centres and enrichment operators.

While the global education sector has seen substantial investment into content platforms and consumer learning applications over the past decade, the operational infrastructure layer supporting the enrichment economy across Southeast Asia remains significantly under-digitised. EdBuddies.ai was built specifically to address this gap.

"Rather than competing within the crowded consumer EdTech landscape, EdBuddies.ai is positioning itself as: Mission-Critical Infrastructure for Education Operators."

The Problem

Southeast Asia's Enrichment Sector is Massively Under-Digitised

The tuition and enrichment industry across Southeast Asia represents a large and structurally resilient market — yet many operators continue to rely on fragmented, manual tools. These inefficiencies directly impact scalability, profitability, parent experience, and service quality.

📊

Spreadsheet Dependency

Student data, billing records, and attendance managed through disconnected spreadsheets with no integration.

🧾

Manual Invoicing

Time-intensive manual invoice creation, tracking, and follow-up with no automated payment workflows.

💬

Fragmented Communication

Parent-teacher communication scattered across WhatsApp, email, and SMS with no unified system.

📅

Disconnected Scheduling

Class timetables managed in isolation, with no integration to billing, attendance, or communications.

✅

Attendance Gaps

Paper-based attendance systems with no real-time visibility for operators or parents.

📈

Rising Expectations

Parents and regulators now expect digital transparency, responsiveness and operational efficiency at every touchpoint.
